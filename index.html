The One, the Only Drake

Biography

Aubrey Drake Graham was born on October 24, 1986 in Toronto, Canada. Drake first started his entertainment career in "Degrassi: The Next Generation". Later, he entered the music business signing with Lil Wayne's label "Young Money Entertainment".

After signing, he made many popular hits including his songs, "Hotline Bling", "Fake Love", "God's Plan", and many more.

You can read more about Drake at https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drake_(musician)
